Spaghetti Carbonara

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan.
  4. Toss spaghetti with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately.
